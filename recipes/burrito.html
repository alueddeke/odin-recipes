Today we are going to be making a spicy burrito. Follow these steps!

Ingredients

  • ground pork

Steps

  1. unpack,open and wash all ingredients
  2. warm up pan to medium heat

  6. this is why you did all of this. Now...you EAT
  7. whatever you can't eat, throw in a container
  8. gently place container in fridge

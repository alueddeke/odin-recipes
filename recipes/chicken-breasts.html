Chimken Broosts

Time to make the Chicken

This next recipe is a personal favorite of mine. I have relied on this method for years and used it just last night!

Ingredients

  • whatever chicken breast you can afford
  • some kind of cooking oil
  • herbs and spices (I used Italian spices and paprika last night)
  • love

Steps

  1. heat oven to 425 degrees
  2. unpack chicken
  3. place parchment paper on a pan, oil her up real good, then toss chicken on parchment paper
  4. do not be afraid to put a lot of spice on these breasts. Take what you have and smother the chicken
  5. put in oven for 22 minutes
  6. EAT
  7. whatever you can't eat, throw in a container
  8. gently place container in fridge
